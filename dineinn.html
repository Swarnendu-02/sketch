Share a Taste of Home with Dine Inn. Codigo partnered Dine Inn as the technical and design consultant to build a platform that bridges hosts and guests to enjoy good food and good times together. Anyone can simply order home-cooked meals via our mobile apps and responsive website 24/7. An intricate logic is implemented to facilitate and accommodate various service types, booking dates & timings, payment between hosts and guests. A fully customised admin panel is also created for the Dine Inn team to manage hosts, guests and bookings to ensure everyone enjoys a great Dine Inn experience.

Key Features One-stop community connecting makers and lovers of food, Buy, sell, pickup, delivery or catering, Stripe integrated to facilitate split payment to all hosts and Dine Inn
